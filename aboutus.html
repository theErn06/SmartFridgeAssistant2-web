About The Project

The Team

TARUMT

We are Year 3 students pursuing a Bachelor Degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering (E&E) at Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology (TARUMT). This project represents our final Capstone Project, showcasing the integration of hardware, software, and artificial intelligence.

Smart Refrigerator Assistant

Our system, affectionately named "Jarvis", is a Smart Refrigerator Assistant designed to revolutionize how households manage food. At its core, it utilizes Edge-LLM technology, allowing for powerful, offline voice control that respects user privacy and works without constant internet dependency for processing commands.

Key Capabilities

  • Offline Voice Control: Add or remove items simply by speaking to the fridge.
  • Remote Sync: Automatically syncs inventory and expiry dates to your phone for access anywhere.
  • Expiry Tracking: Monitors fresh ingredients to alert you before food goes bad.

Our Mission

By providing intelligent monitoring of fresh ingredients, this kit helps households achieve three main goals:

  • Significantly reduce food waste.
  • Save money on unnecessary groceries.
  • Minimize environmental impact.
